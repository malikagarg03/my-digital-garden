**What this is:** Influence — changing what people choose to do without directly compelling them, whether the target is the public or your own personnel.

## Notes

Example techniques (not yet written up as their own notes): negotiation, persuasion, warning, reassurance, media communication, community engagement, visible policing, flag marches — plus, on the personnel side, coaching, mentoring, and recognition.
