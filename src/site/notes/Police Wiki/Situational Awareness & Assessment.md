**What this is:** Understand — interpreting and testing what you know: what's actually happening, and is it true?

## Techniques that serve this tactic

## Notes

Example techniques (not yet written up as their own notes): case theory, threat assessment, corroboration, timeline reconstruction, reading crowd mood, analysing intelligence.

This is where a case theory gets tested rather than just formed — corroboration, checking for alternative explanations, and resolving contradictions all live here, whether the subject is a criminal case or an intelligence report about a possible clash.
