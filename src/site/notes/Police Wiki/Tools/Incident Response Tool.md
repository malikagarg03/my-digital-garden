**What this is:** A web-based prompt that helps an officer slow down and structure their thinking during an unfolding incident, before acting.

**Used for:** Supports [[Police Wiki/Situations/Something Happened — What Should I Do?\|Something Happened — What Should I Do?]] directly, and more generally the [[Police Wiki/Tactics/Situational Awareness & Assessment\|Situational Awareness & Assessment]] and [[Police Wiki/Tactics/Planning & Decision-Making\|Planning & Decision-Making]] tactics — anywhere the job is to organise your thoughts under pressure rather than react on instinct.

## Notes

👉 [⚡ Incident Response Tool](https://malikagarg.xyz/prompt-library/incident-response.html)

Needs a network connection to load, so it isn't usable if you're somewhere without connectivity — worth having the core steps memorised or printed as a backup (see [[Police Wiki/Situations/Something Happened — What Should I Do?\|Something Happened — What Should I Do?]] for the same steps written out directly).
